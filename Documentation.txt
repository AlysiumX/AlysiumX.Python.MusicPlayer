This is a music player.

Features -
    -Select songs, and play and stop.
    -Repeat 1|All
    -Random
    -Save Playlist
    -Always on top.

    -Auto Albums based on folder structure.
    -Definable Albums - This will allow you to create albums by creating a new one with a selection of songs. This will
    be added to the albums library with or without a picture.

    -Song play tracking - Number of times played.
